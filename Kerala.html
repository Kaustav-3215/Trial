Kerala Plan

This 5-day Kerala tour blends cultural immersion, natural beauty, and tranquil backwaters, covering Kochi, Munnar, Thekkady, and Alleppey. Explore Kochi’s historic sites, unwind in Munnar’s tea plantations, experience wildlife in Thekkady’s Periyar National Park, and relax on a houseboat cruise through Alleppey’s serene backwaters. This itinerary promises an unforgettable journey through Kerala’s rich heritage and landscapes.

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

  • Morning : Arrive at Cochin International Airport and transfer to Fort Kochi (approx. taxi fare: ₹1,200–₹1,500).
  • Afternoon : Explore the historic Santa Cruz Basilica (free) and the vibrant lanes of Jew Town, known for antique shops and the Paradesi Synagogue(entry: ₹10–₹20).
  • Evening : Attend a traditional Kathakali dance performance at a renowned center in Fort Kochi (ticket: ₹500 per person).
  • Accommodation : Overnight at a comfortable mid-range hotel in Kochi. Prices typically range from ₹1,200 to ₹3,000 per night for a double room in a well-rated property.

Day 2: Transfer to Munnar

  • Morning : Scenic drive to Munnar (approx. 4 hours, taxi fare: ₹3,500–₹4,500). Stop at Cheeyappara and Valara Waterfalls(free) and offer beautiful photo opportunities.
  • Afternoon : Visit Eravikulam National Park (entry: ₹200 per person) to spot the endangered Nilgiri tahr, then tour the Munnar Tea Museum (entry: ₹125 per person).
  • Evening : Enjoy sunset views from Karadiparra Viewpoint (free).
  • Accommodation : Stay at a tea estate resort in Munnar. Mid-range resorts cost ₹3,000–₹5,000 per night.

Day 3: Munnar Sightseeing

  • Morning : Trek through lush tea plantations (guided walks: ₹500–₹1,000 per person), visit Mattupetty Dam (entry: ₹10 per person), and Echo Point (entry: ₹30 per person).
  • Afternoon : Explore the serene Kundala Lake (boating: ₹100–₹300 per person).
  • Evening : Optional campfire or cultural activities at the resort (often included or ₹500–₹1,000 extra).
  • Accommodation : Continue at the same Munnar resort.

Day 4: Thekkady Wildlife Experience

  • Morning : Drive to Thekkady (approx. 4 hours, taxi fare: ₹3,000–₹4,000). Check into a jungle-facing eco-resort (₹3,000–₹5,000 per night).
  • Afternoon : Take a boat safari on Periyar Lake to spot elephants and birds (₹450 per person).
  • Evening : Choose between a guided spice plantation tour (₹200–₹500 per person) or a Kalaripayattu martial arts show (₹500 per person).
  • Accommodation : Overnight at a Thekkady eco-resort.

Day 5: Alleppey Backwaters & Departure

  • Morning : Transfer to Alleppey (approx. 4 hours, taxi fare: ₹3,000–₹4,000). Board a private houseboat for a day cruise (₹7,000–₹10,000 for two people, including meals).
  • Afternoon : Cruise through the tranquil Vembanad Lake, savoring Kerala cuisine and village life.
  • Evening : Disembark and drive to Kochi for your departure (taxi fare: ₹2,500–₹3,000).
