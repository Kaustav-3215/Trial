Rajasthan Plan

Embark on a 5-day journey through Rajasthan, exploring the royal grandeur of Jaipur, the spiritual aura of Pushkar, and the romantic charm of Udaipur. This itinerary seamlessly weaves together majestic forts, sacred temples, bustling bazaars, vibrant folk culture, and tranquil lakeside moments for a truly unforgettable experience. Discover why Rajasthan is celebrated as “The Incredible State of India”.

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

  • Morning : Arrive at Jaipur International Airport and transfer to your comfortable mid-range hotel (taxi: ₹800–₹1,200; hotel: ₹3,000–₹5,000 per night).
  • Afternoon : Enjoy the magnificent Amber Fort, intricate mirrorwork of Sheesh Mahal(entry: ₹25), and picturesque views of Jal Mahal from the lakeside (entry: ₹50–₹100).
  • Evening : Stroll past the iconic façade of Hawa Mahal (entry: ₹50) and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Johari Bazaar, famed for its jewelry and handicrafts.
  • Accommodation : Overnight at a mid-range hotel in Jaipur (₹3,000–₹5,000 per night).

Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing

  • Morning : Discover the regal City Palace (entry: ₹300) and the astronomical wonders of Jantar Mantar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site (entry: ₹50).
  • Afternoon : Visit the impressive Albert Hall Museum (entry: ₹40) and the serene Birla Temple (free). Enjoy a delicious lunch at a local eatery (₹400–₹800 per person).
  • Evening : Experience Rajasthan’s vibrant culture with a folk performance and traditional dinner at Chokhi Dhani (entry & dinner: ₹900–₹1,200 per person).
  • Accommodation : Stay at the same hotel in Jaipur.

Day 3: Jaipur → Pushkar → Udaipur

  • Morning : Journey to Pushkar (taxi: ₹2,500–₹3,500), visit the sacred Brahma Mandir (special poojas ₹100–₹500), and witness rituals at the tranquil Pushkar Lake (free).
  • Afternoon : Continue to Udaipur (taxi: ₹4,000–₹5,000) and check into a heritage hotel (₹4,000–₹8,000 per night).
  • Evening : Savor a magical sunset boat ride on Lake Pichola (₹400–₹700 per person), with breathtaking views of the Lake Palace shimmering on the water.
  • Accommodation : Overnight at a heritage hotel in Udaipur (₹4,000–₹8,000 per night).

Day 4: Udaipur Sightseeing

  • Morning : Explore the grand City Palace (entry: ₹300), visit the historic Jagdish Temple (entry: free), and wander through the lush Saheliyon Ki Bari gardens (entry: ₹30).
  • Afternoon : Enjoy the scenic beauty of Fateh Sagar Lake (entry: free, boating extra) and dine at a rooftop restaurant with panoramic views (₹500–₹1,000 per person).
  • Evening : Attend a classical dance show or indulge in a lakeside dinner (show: ₹300–₹500 per person; dinner: ₹800–₹1,500 per person).
  • Accommodation : Stay at the same heritage hotel.

Day 5: Departure from Udaipur

  • Morning : Visit Sajjangarh Palace (Monsoon Palace) for sweeping views of Udaipur and the Aravalli hills (entry: ₹110).
  • Afternoon : Enjoy the scenic drive and a farewell lunch on the way to the airport (lunch: ₹500–₹1,000 per person).
  • Evening : Transfer to Udaipur Airport for your departure (taxi: ₹800–₹1,200).
