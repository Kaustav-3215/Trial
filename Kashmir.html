Jammu and Kashmir Plan

Embark on a 5-day Kashmir tour that takes you through the breathtaking destinations of Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Sonamarg. This itinerary perfectly blends natural beauty, adventure, and cultural experiences. Enjoy serene shikara rides, scenic mountain vistas, and a taste of authentic Kashmiri hospitality, making it an unforgettable getaway.

Day 1 : Arrival in Srinagar

  • Morning : Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to your hotel or houseboat (taxi approx. ₹1,200). Hotels range from ₹1,700–₹5,500/night; houseboats start at ₹3,000/night.
  • Afternoon : Visit the Mughal Gardens (entry ₹20–₹50 per person) and enjoy the beautiful views and historic atmosphere.
  • Evening : Take a relaxing Shikara ride on Dal Lake (₹790/hour for up to 4 people).
  • Accommodation : Overnight stay in a Srinagar hotel (₹1,700–₹5,500/night) or houseboat (₹3,000–₹7,000/night).

Day 2 : Gulmarg Day Trip

  • Morning : Drive 50km from Srinagar to Gulmarg, covering in about 1.5 to 2 hours. Taxi fares for this route start around ₹3,000 for a sedan and ₹3,700 for an Innova.
  • Afternoon : Take the famous Gulmarg Gondola ride to Khilanmarg and enjoy the stunning panoramic views. The Gondola ticket costs ₹810 for Phase 1, ₹1,010 for Phase 2.
  • Evening : Return to Srinagar by taxi, with fares similar to the onward trip (₹3,000–₹3,700). Relax and unwind after a day of scenic exploration.
  • Accommodation : Overnight stay in a Srinagar hotel (₹1,700–₹5,500 per night) or a houseboat on Dal Lake (starting from ₹3,000 per night), for an unique experience.

Day 3 : Pahalgam Day Trip

  • Morning : Drive to Pahalgam (approx. 90 km, 2.5 hours by taxi; fare ₹2,600–₹3,700 for a sedan). Enjoy scenic views of saffron fields and the Lidder River along the way.
  • Afternoon : Visit Betaab Valley (entry ₹100/person), explore Aru Valley (cab ₹2,000), and enjoy activities like horse riding (₹1,320/person at Baisaran, ₹600–₹800 at Aru).
  • Evening : Return to Srinagar by taxi (return fare is double, or about ₹5,200–₹7,400 for a sedan round trip). Relax and reflect on your day’s adventure.
  • Accommodation : Overnight stay in a Srinagar hotel (₹1,700–₹5,500/night) or houseboat (₹3,000–₹7,000/night) for a comfortable and memorable experience.

Day 4 : Sonamarg Day Trip

  • Morning : Drive 80km from Srinagar to Sonamarg, around 2 hours. Taxi fares range from ₹2,267 to ₹4,000 one way depending on vehicle type.
  • Afternoon : Visit the stunning Thajiwas Glacier, accessible by a 2-3 hour trek or pony ride. Pony rides cost between ₹700 and ₹1,500 depending on the season.
  • Evening : Return to Srinagar by taxi with similar fares as the onward trip. Relax after a day of scenic exploration.
  • Accommodation : Overnight stay in a Srinagar hotel or houseboat, with prices ranging from ₹1,700 to ₹7,000 per night depending on your choice.

Day 5 : Srinagar and Departure

  • Morning : Visit Shankaracharya Temple (no entry fee) for panoramic views. Shop for souvenirs at local markets like Lal Chowk or the Floating Market.
  • Afternoon : On the way back to the airport, have lunch at a local restaurant, with meals costing between ₹300-₹800 per person. Enjoy the scenic beauty of the surroundings.
  • Evening : Take a taxi transfer to Srinagar Airport for your departure. Taxi fare from the city center to the airport is approximately ₹1,200.
