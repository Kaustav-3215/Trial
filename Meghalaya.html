Meghalaya Plan

This 5-day Meghalaya itinerary immerses you in the state’s natural wonders-crystal-clear rivers, ancient living root bridges, and mist-shrouded highlands. Experience the vibrant culture and breathtaking landscapes of Shillong, Cherrapunji, Mawlynnong, and Dawki for an unforgettable adventure in the Abode of Clouds.

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati → Transfer to Shillong

  • Morning : Arrive at Guwahati Airport or Railway Station and enjoy a picturesque drive to Shillong (private taxi: ₹2,500–₹4,000; shared cab/bus: ₹700–₹1,200 per person).
  • Afternoon : Check into your hotel (₹2,000–₹4,000 per night). Explore the serene Ward’s Lake (entry: ₹20) and fascinating exhibits at Don Bosco Museum (entry: ₹150).
  • Evening : Dive into the buzz of Police Bazaar, browsing local handicrafts and sampling authentic Khasi cuisine (dinner: ₹300–₹600 per person).
  • Accommodation : Mid-range hotel in Shillong (₹2,000–₹4,000 per night).

Day 2: Shillong → Cherrapunji (Sohra)

  • Morning : A scenic drive to Cherrapunji (taxi: ₹2,500–₹3,500), stopping at Mawkdok Dympep Valley for an exhilarating zip-lining experience (₹400–₹800 per person).
  • Afternoon : Witness the amazing Nohkalikai Falls (entry: ₹30–₹50), the mysterious Mawsmai Cave (entry: ₹20–₹40) and the panoramic Seven Sisters Falls (entry: ₹30).
  • Evening : Check into a welcoming homestay in Cherrapunji (₹1,500–₹3,000 per night) and unwind amidst the misty hills.
  • Accommodation : Homestay in Cherrapunji.

Day 3: Living Root Bridge Trek (Nongriat)

  • Morning : Embark on a memorable 3,500 steps trek through lush forest from Tyrna village to the iconic Double Decker Living Root Bridge (entry: ₹50; guide: ₹600).
  • Afternoon : Optionally, continue to the enchanting Rainbow Falls (additional guide: ₹300–₹500). Return to Cherrapunji by evening.
  • Evening : Relax at your homestay or explore the local markets for souvenirs and snacks.
  • Accommodation : Same homestay in Cherrapunji.

Day 4: Cherrapunji → Mawlynnong → Dawki → Shillong

  • Morning : Drive to Mawlynnong, Asia’s cleanest village (taxi: ₹3,000–₹4,000), and visit the Riwai Living Root Bridge (entry: ₹20–₹30).
  • Afternoon : Continue to Dawki for a magical boat ride on the crystal-clear Umngot River (boating: ₹800–₹1,200 per boat) and a visit to the Indo-Bangladesh border (free).
  • Evening : Return to Shillong (taxi: ₹3,000–₹4,000) and relax at your hotel.
  • Accommodation : Mid-range hotel in Shillong (₹2,000–₹4,000 per night).

Day 5: Shillong → Guwahati Departure

  • Morning : Take in the breathtaking views at Laitlum Canyons (entry: free), a perfect finale to your Meghalaya adventure.
  • Afternoon : Enjoy a scenic drive back to Guwahati, with a leisurely lunch at a local restaurant en route (lunch: ₹300–₹600 per person).
  • Evening : Depart from Guwahati Airport or Railway Station, carrying home memories of Meghalaya’s magic.
