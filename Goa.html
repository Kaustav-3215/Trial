Goa Plan

This 5-day Goa itinerary combines sun-soaked beaches, vibrant culture, adventure, and relaxation. Explore the highlights of North and South Goa, from lively markets and historic sites to thrilling water sports and the laid-back charm of Old Goa and Panaji. Each day offers a perfect mix of sightseeing and leisure. Whether you're a beach lover, history buff, foodie, or adventure seeker, this well-rounded trip promises unforgettable memories.

Day 1: Arrival & Beach Time

  • Morning : Arrive in Goa and check into your Calangute/Baga hotel or guesthouse (taxi fare: ₹1,200–₹1,800).
  • Afternoon : Relax and sunbathe at Calangute Beach, enjoy the lively beach atmosphere and water activities (water sports: ₹500–₹1,500 per person).
  • Evening : Watch the sunset and have dinner at a Baga Beach shack, sampling Goan seafood (meal: ₹400–₹800 per person).
  • Accommodation : Stay in a budget hotel/guesthouse in Calangute/Baga (₹1,000–₹3,500 per night).

Day 2: North Goa Exploration

  • Morning : Visit Anjuna Beach and explore the famous flea market (shopping at your own pace).
  • Afternoon : Lunch at a local café, then visit Chapora Fort for panoramic views and Vagator Beach for relaxation (entry free).
  • Evening : Enjoy sunset at Vagator and dinner at a nearby café or shack (meal: ₹400–₹800 per person).
  • Accommodation : Stay in the same Calangute/Baga hotel.

Day 3: South Goa Serenity

  • Morning : Drive to Palolem Beach in South Goa (taxi fare: ₹2,000–₹3,000). Relax on the scenic, palm-fringed beach.
  • Afternoon : Lunch at a Palolem beach shack, then visit Cabo de Rama Fort for history and sea views (entry free).
  • Evening : Return to North Goa and unwind at your hotel.
  • Accommodation : Stay in the same Calangute/Baga hotel.

Day 4: Culture & Old Goa

  • Morning : Visit Old Goa’s Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral (UNESCO World Heritage Sites; entry: ₹10–₹50 per site).
  • Afternoon : Explore the colorful Fontainhas Latin Quarter and have lunch in Panaji (meal: ₹400–₹700 per person).
  • Evening : Shop at Panaji market or enjoy a Mandovi River sunset cruise (cruise: ₹500–₹1,000 per person).
  • Accommodation : Stay in the same Calangute/Baga hotel.

Day 5: Water Sports & Departure

  • Morning : Enjoy water sports at Calangute or Candolim Beach (parasailing, jet skiing, banana boat; ₹500–₹1,500 per activity).
  • Afternoon : Lunch at a beach shack and last-minute shopping for souvenirs and local crafts.
  • Evening : Check out and depart for the airport or railway station (taxi fare: ₹1,200–₹1,800).
