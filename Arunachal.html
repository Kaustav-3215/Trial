Arunachal Pradesh Plan

This 5-day Arunachal Pradesh expedition takes you through the pristine Himalayan landscapes of Bhalukpong, Dirang, Tawang, and Bomdila. Journey through snow-capped mountains, visit ancient Buddhist monasteries, and immerse yourself in the unique cultural heritage of India's northeastern frontier. Enjoy scenic drives, vibrant markets, serene rivers, and natural wonders along the way.

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati/Tezpur → Bhalukpong

  • Morning : Arrive at Guwahati or Tezpur airport and embark on a scenic drive to Bhalukpong (private taxi: ₹4,000–₹6,000).
  • Afternoon : Settle into your comfortable budget hotel (₹2,000–₹3,000 per night). Explore the renowned Tipi Orchidarium, with over 500 orchid species(entry: ₹40–₹100).
  • Evening : Unwind by the crystal-clear Kameng River or browse through local markets for authentic tribal crafts.
  • Accommodation : Cozy budget hotel in Bhalukpong (₹2,000–₹3,000 per night).

Day 2: Bhalukpong → Dirang

  • Morning : Journey to Dirang, with a memorable stop at Bomdila Viewpoint for breathtaking Himalayan panoramas (taxi: ₹3,000–₹4,000).
  • Afternoon : Discover the historic Dirang Dzong (entry: free), wander through the picturesque Sangti Valley, and rejuvenate at the natural Hot Water Springs (entry: free).
  • Evening : Check into a comfortable mid-range hotel and savor local Monpa cuisine.
  • Accommodation : Charming mid-range hotel in Dirang (₹2,500–₹3,500 per night).

Day 3: Dirang → Tawang via Sela Pass

  • Morning : Ascend the legendary Sela Pass (13,700 ft), marvel at the serene Sela Lake (entry: free), and pay respects at the Jaswant Garh War Memorial (entry: free).
  • Afternoon : Be mesmerized by the thundering Nuranang Falls (entry: free) before continuing to Tawang (taxi: ₹4,000–₹5,000).
  • Evening : Visit the magnificent 400-year-old Tawang Monastery and the imposing Giant Buddha Statue as the golden hour illuminates the mountains.
  • Accommodation : Well-appointed mid-range hotel in Tawang (₹2,000–₹3,000 per night).

Day 4: Tawang Sightseeing

  • Morning : Excursion to Bum La Pass at the Indo-China border (permit required, local vehicle: ₹4,000–₹5,000) and the enchanting Madhuri Lake.
  • Afternoon : Honor fallen heroes at the Tawang War Memorial (entry: free) and browse exquisite local handicrafts at the Craft Centre.
  • Evening : Immerse yourself in Tawang's vibrant market culture, shopping for Tibetan treasures and savoring steaming momos.
  • Accommodation : Same welcoming hotel in Tawang (₹2,000–₹3,000 per night).

Day 5: Tawang → Bomdila → Guwahati/Tezpur

  • Morning : Descend to Bomdila, visiting the tranquil Bomdila Monastery with its stunning architecture and spiritual ambiance (taxi: ₹4,000–₹5,000).
  • Afternoon : Enjoy a farewell lunch featuring regional specialties in Bomdila (meal: ₹300–₹600), then continue your journey to Guwahati or Tezpur (taxi: ₹6,000–₹8,000).
  • Evening : Depart from Guwahati/Tezpur airport with memories to last a lifetime.
